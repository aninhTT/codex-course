# Lesson 4.03: Make A Guided Prototype Change

## Learn

The first prototype edit should be focused. The learner is not building a full product yet. They are making one meaningful change to see how Codex can modify a small app.

Best practice: make the change, preview it, and review what changed before expanding the idea.

## Practice

Try this in Codex:

```text
Turn this starter app into a cleaner personal work dashboard. Keep it simple and local. Add one section for "This Week's Focus", improve the wording, and keep the design intentional rather than generic. After editing, summarize what changed and how I should preview it.
```

**Success criteria:** The app gains one focused improvement, and the learner knows how to preview and review it.
