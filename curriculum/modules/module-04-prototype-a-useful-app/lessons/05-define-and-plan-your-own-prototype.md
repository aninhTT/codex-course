# Lesson 4.05: Define And Plan Your Own Prototype

## Learn

Now the learner chooses a prototype idea they actually want to build. Keep the first version small: 2 to 3 features, a clear user, and a clear job.

A good v1 plan also says what is out of scope. Leaving things out on purpose keeps the build understandable.

## Practice

Try this in Codex:

```text
Help me define and plan a small prototype based on one of my workflow ideas. Ask me a few short questions, recommend a realistic v1 with only 2 to 3 features, name the folder or project where it should live, and list what we should leave out for now. Do not build anything yet.
```

**Success criteria:** The learner has a small v1 prototype plan, a chosen project location, and clear out-of-scope boundaries.
