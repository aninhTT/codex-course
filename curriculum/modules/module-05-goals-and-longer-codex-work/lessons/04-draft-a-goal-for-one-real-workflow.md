# Lesson 5.04: Draft A Goal For One Real Workflow

## Learn

Before activating a Goal, ask Codex to help shape it. The interview should answer what outcome you want, what evidence would prove it, what sources Codex can use, what to avoid, what counts as done, and what counts as blocked.

That interview step is the learner's steering wheel. It keeps Goal mode useful without turning it into vague momentum.

## Practice

Try this in Codex:

```text
Help me turn one real workflow into a strong Codex Goal. Interview me first. Then draft a Goal that includes the desired outcome, evidence source, project or folder context, constraints, allowed inputs, boundaries, and blocked stop condition. Do not activate the Goal yet.
```

**Success criteria:** The learner ends with a Goal draft that is specific enough to verify and clear enough to review before activation.
