# Lesson 6.03: Resume After Working Elsewhere

## Learn

When the learner returns to the course project, Codex may not automatically know what happened in another project. Bring the handoff summary back.

A good resume prompt says where you were, what you did elsewhere, what changed, and what lesson you want to continue.

This makes project switching feel like a normal workflow instead of a detour.

## Practice

Try this in Codex:

```text
I'm back in the course project. Based on this summary, help me resume the course: [paste my project-switching summary here]. Tell me what lesson I am on, what artifact I should have, and the next sensible step.
```

**Success criteria:** The learner knows how to resume the course after working in another project.
