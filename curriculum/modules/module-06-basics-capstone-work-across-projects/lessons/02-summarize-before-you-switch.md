# Lesson 6.02: Summarize Before You Switch

## Learn

Switching projects is normal. Losing your place is optional.

Before leaving the course project, ask Codex to summarize what happened, what changed, what project you are switching to, and where you should return. That summary becomes your handoff note.

This is useful for skills, prototypes, Goals, and later power-user modules.

## Practice

Try this in Codex:

```text
Before I switch projects, summarize what we just did, what files or artifacts changed, what project or folder I am switching to next, and what lesson I should return to afterward. Keep it short enough that I can paste it back into Codex later.
```

**Success criteria:** The learner has a reusable summary prompt and a short handoff note format.
