# Lesson 6.03: Break Down a Larger Task

## Learn

Real work is often bigger than a one-shot prompt. This lesson helps the learner map a use case across different artifact types instead of forcing everything into one solution.

## Practice

Try this in Codex:

```text
Take one of my chosen use cases and break it into a practical Codex plan. Show me what could become an improvement to my starter skill, what could become a bigger custom workflow skill, and what might be better as a lightweight app or dashboard.
```

**Success criteria:** The learner ends with a clear division of labor between different skill ideas and the app, rather than a vague all-purpose plan.
