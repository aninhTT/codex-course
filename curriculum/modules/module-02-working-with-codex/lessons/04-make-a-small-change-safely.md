# Lesson 2.04: Make a Small Change Safely

## Learn

This lesson is the first transition from read-only help to a real edit. The task is intentionally narrow so the learner can focus on the edit-and-review loop rather than on complexity.

Best practice: when you are new to Codex, ask for the smallest useful change and ask Codex to summarize what changed afterward. That gives you something real to review without turning the exercise into a mystery.

## Practice

Try this in Codex:

```text
Rewrite `practice-workspace/notes/weekly-review-draft.md` into a cleaner weekly recap using `practice-workspace/templates/weekly-recap-template.md`. Keep the tone practical and concise. Make the smallest useful change, then tell me what changed and what I should review.
```

**Success criteria:** Codex makes a focused improvement to the file, explains what changed, and the learner reviews the result before accepting it as complete.
